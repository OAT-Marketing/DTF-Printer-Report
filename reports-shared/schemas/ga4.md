# ga4.html CSV schema

Files live in `DTFJersey/reports/data/`.

## ga4-monthly.csv
Columns: `month,total_users,sessions,engaged_sessions,new_users`
Example: `Apr 2026,340295,351544,174583,224000`

## ga4-channels.csv
Columns: `channel,month,total_users,sessions,engaged_sessions,new_users,share_pct`
Example: `Organic Search,Apr 2026,12000,15000,9000,8000,3.5`

## ga4-landing.csv
Columns: `page,channel,month,sessions,total_users,engaged_sessions`
Example: `/collections/dtf-transfers,Organic Search,Apr 2026,1200,950,700`
